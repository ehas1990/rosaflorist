About Us

Rosa Florist & Decor is a renowned name in the floristry industry in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. Established in 2000, our company began its journey on a very small scale and today. We have grown to our current dimensions with hard work and dedication. The core belief that customer satisfaction is as important as their products and services has helped us garner a vast base of customers, which continues to grow by the day. Our team is dedicated and aims to achieve the company's goal.


Our packaging is creative as well, ensuring freshness and long-lasting appeal of ordered flowers. The objective of our company is to achieve quality products at competitive prices. We're here to help you find just the right way to celebrate any occasion.

Let us do the work for you and make every occasion or day special with beautiful fresh floral arrangements or gifts. So whether you're looking for roses or something completely different, we'll take care of the details while you take a deep breath and enjoy the moment. Through this memorable moment, we want you to cherish every bit of it – from its beautiful design to your loved ones' beaming face, making for a truly unforgettable moment.
